💥 Greatest Knockouts Ever

A collection of some of the most iconic, violent, and legendary knockouts in boxing history.

🥊 Mike Tyson vs Trevor Berbick (1986)

One of the most famous early knockouts of Mike Tyson’s career. He became the youngest heavyweight champion in history.

🥊 George Foreman vs Michael Moorer (1994)

Foreman shocked the world by becoming heavyweight champion again at age 45. A legendary comeback KO.

🥊 Manny Pacquiao vs Ricky Hatton (2009)

A devastating left hook that ended the fight instantly in round 2. One of Pacquiao’s most iconic wins.

🥊 Deontay Wilder vs Luis Ortiz (2018)

A brutal comeback KO after being behind on the scorecards. Wilder’s power changed the fight instantly.

🥊 Sugar Ray Robinson vs Gene Fullmer (1957)

A perfect left hook knockout considered one of the most beautiful finishes ever.

🥊 Joe Louis vs Max Schmeling II (1938)

A historic first-round knockout with massive global significance.

🥊 Canelo Alvarez vs James Kirkland (2015)

A highlight-reel combination ending with a brutal right hand KO.

🥊 Muhammad Ali vs Sonny Liston II (1965)

The famous “phantom punch” knockout that shocked the world.

💥 What Makes a Great Knockout?

Timing, power, and importance. The biggest knockouts change careers, titles, and history.
