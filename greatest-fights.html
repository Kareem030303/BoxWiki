🏆 Greatest Fights in Boxing History

Ali vs Frazier I (1971)

The Fight of the Century. Frazier won by unanimous decision.

Ali vs Foreman (1974)

The famous Rumble in the Jungle. Ali stopped Foreman in Round 8.

Hagler vs Hearns (1985)

One of the most explosive fights ever, ending in Round 3.

Gatti vs Ward I (2002)

A legendary action fight that thrilled fans.

Fury vs Wilder III (2021)

An epic heavyweight battle featuring multiple knockdowns.
