🏆 Championship Belt Encyclopedia

🟢 WBC (World Boxing Council)

Founded in 1963, the WBC is one of the most prestigious organizations in boxing. Its championship belt is famous for its green color.

🔵 WBA (World Boxing Association)

Founded in 1921, the WBA is the oldest major boxing sanctioning body. Many legendary champions have held WBA titles.

🔴 IBF (International Boxing Federation)

Founded in 1983, the IBF is known for strict rankings and mandatory challengers.

🟡 WBO (World Boxing Organization)

Founded in 1988, the WBO became a major world title recognized across boxing.

⚫ Ring Magazine Championship

The Ring title is awarded by Ring Magazine and is often called "The Lineal Championship" when the best fights the best.

👑 Undisputed Champion

An undisputed champion holds all four major world titles: WBC, WBA, IBF, and WBO.
